Careers Owner Operator

Be the Master of
Your Own Destiny.

Owning your truck outright can be quite intimidating as you never know if your capital investment will reap steady profits. Nova Lines takes your worries away by partnering with you to make your investment a worthwhile venture and help you build a robust foundation for your trucking business.

For those who just want to test the waters, Nova Lines offers a 'Pathway to Ownership' program enabling you to develop an understanding of what it takes to own your truck without the risk of purchasing first then learning the hard way.

Owner Operator Benefits

  • Steady revenue helps keep your investment safe and your business growing
  • Get access to an efficient operational team and consistent loads day in day out
  • Build and grow your trucking business with brand new trailers
  • Maintain positive cash flow with weekly direct deposits
  • Safety Bonuses
  • Health Insurance

Driver Eligibility

  • Must have Class A CDL
  • 2 Years of OTR Experience
  • No preventable accidents in the last 18 months
  • No DUIs in the last 5 years
  • No more than 3 moving violations in the last 3 years

Hometime Options

Nova Lines gives the power of choice back to the drivers by allowing them to choose their own routes and distances.

As an Owner Operator, you choose your routes and distances that allow you to balance your personal and professional lives. Our pool of most successful contractors chooses to go home at least once every 2 to 3 weeks.

Owner Operator FAQs

Who pays for tolls?

Contractors are liable for paying their tolls. On the other hand, our trucks are equipped with Prepass Elite Pass to ensure convenience and quick bypasses.

What is Nova Lines’ home time policy?

As an Owner Operator, you choose your routes and distances that allow you to balance your personal and professional lives. Our pool of most successful contractors chooses to go home at least once every 2 to 3 weeks.

Does Nova Lines accept any truck into its fleet?

Unfortunately, no. We have committed to maintaining a high level of efficiency, performance, and safety which cannot be warranted by having trucks that have already served their time. This is why we only accept truck makes and models that are not older than 10 years.

Do I need my own physical damage insurance?

Not necessarily. You can buy physical damage insurance by paying 5% of the truck value. This does not only provides you with a financial cover but also ensures peace of mind.

If I own my truck, is it mandatory to be governed?

One of the perks of being an owner-operator is that get to choose how your truck runs. However, it is advised to ensure your vehicle and your own safety.

Do I need my own trailer?

Not necessarily. You can rent a trailer from us starting from $250 weekly while we also accept trailers into our fleet that have been manufactured in the last 10 years at the time of contract.

What is the revenue percentage with Nova Lines?

At Nova Lines, we firmly believe that our success is in your success. To ensure you establish financial stability, we pay our contractors 80% of what is billed to the customer.

Does Nova Line offer a lease purchase program?

Absolutely! Nova Lines offers a flexible and driver-friendly lease purchase program to qualified candidates enabling them to move towards self-employment and gain financial independence.
